About me

With a passion for technology, I aspire to excel in Software Engineering and leverage my skills to serve my community in meaningful ways. I believe that through innovation and collaboration, I can contribute positively to the development of my society and inspire others along the way.

Educational Background

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering at Addis Ababa University, one of Ethiopia's leading institutions for higher education, located in the vibrant capital city of Addis Ababa. My expected graduation date is in 2027, and I am dedicated to acquiring a comprehensive understanding of software development, programming languages, and system design. Throughout my academic journey, I have consistently strived for excellence, which was recognized when I received an award for my outstanding performance on the Grade 12 entrance exam. This achievement not only reflects my hard work and determination but also fuels my passion for technology and innovation.

Looking ahead, I aspire to contribute to innovative software solutions that address local and global challenges. I am committed to continuous learning and professional development, aiming to become a proficient software engineer who can make a meaningful impact in the tech industry.
